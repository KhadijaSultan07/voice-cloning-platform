## 🚀 What Makes This App Special?

### 1. Urdu-First Approach
This is the **first voice cloning platform** designed specifically for **Urdu speakers**. Whether you write in Roman Urdu, Pure Urdu (Nastaliq), or English, the model understands and speaks naturally.

### 2. No Technical Skills Required
- **No coding needed** - Just type and click
- **No setup required** - Works in your browser
- **No installations** - Everything is online

### 3. Professional-Grade Quality
- **48kHz studio-quality audio** - Better than most commercial services
- **Natural prosody** - Voices sound human, not robotic
- **Emotion-aware synthesis** - Text feels alive

### 4. Privacy First
- Your audio is **processed securely**
- No permanent storage of your voice
- Your data stays private

## 📋 Quick Start Guide

### 🌐 Use Online (No Installation)
1. Open the app link
2. Type or paste your text
3. (Optional) Upload reference audio
4. Click **"Generate"**
5. Download your audio

### 💻 Run Locally
```bash
